Class A Office Building — Classification Standard
International classification frameworks (CBRE / Cushman & Wakefield / BOMA International) define Class A office space by a combination of location quality, technical specification, building services, and tenant environment. Each criterion is divided into two levels: Mandatory — the minimum requirement for Class A classification, and Desirable — Class A+ standard or international best practice.

Source: CBRE Georgia Office Market Report 2024 · Cushman & Wakefield Global Office Classification Standards · BOMA International

📍
ადგილმდებარეობა და ხელმისაწვდომობა
● Mandatory
  • Located within the primary business corridor or Central Business District
  • Walking distance to the nearest metro station or direct public transit connection not exceeding 10 minutes
  • Adequate road access and clearly marked vehicle drop-off zone
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Recognized premium business district with proximity to five-star hotels and international banking institutions
  • Strong market visibility from major arterial roads and international brand recognition of the address
📐
სართულის ფართობი და ჭერის სიმაღლე
● Mandatory
  • Minimum net rentable floor area of 1,000 square meters per floor
  • Clear ceiling height of at least 2.7 meters (finished floor to underside of structure)
  • Floor-to-floor height between 3.6 and 4.2 meters to accommodate mechanical, electrical, and plumbing service zones
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Floor plate of 1,500 square meters or more
  • Clear ceiling height of 3.0 meters or greater
  • Raised access flooring (100–150 mm) for flexible underfloor cable routing and air distribution
  • Column-free or minimal-column layout for maximum open-plan flexibility
❄️
გათბობა, ვენტილაცია და კონდიციონირება
● Mandatory
  • Central or individually zoned Variable Refrigerant Flow / Variable Refrigerant Volume climate control system
  • Fresh air supply rate of at least 10 liters per second per person in all occupied zones
  • 24-hour, seven-days-a-week operation under active Building Management System control
  • Temperature maintained at 21–23°C ±1°C; relative humidity between 40 and 60 percent
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Four-pipe fan coil unit system enabling simultaneous independent heating and cooling in different zones
  • Chilled beam technology or underfloor air distribution
  • Real-time carbon dioxide and fine particulate matter (PM2.5) air quality sensors with automated ventilation response
🛗
ლიფტები და ვერტიკალური ტრანსპორტი
● Mandatory
  • Minimum elevator travel speed of 1.6 meters per second
  • At least one elevator per every 5,000 square meters of gross floor area
  • Average passenger waiting time during peak periods not exceeding 30 seconds
  • Equipment from recognized manufacturers: KONE, Otis, Schindler, or Kleemann
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Travel speed of 2.5 meters per second or greater; average waiting time of 20 seconds or less
  • Destination Dispatch System for optimized elevator allocation
  • Dedicated service or freight elevator separate from passenger lifts
🅿️
პარკინგი
● Mandatory
  • Minimum parking ratio of 1 space per 30 to 40 square meters of net rentable area
  • Underground or structured parking (surface-level street parking does not count)
  • Continuous 24-hour closed-circuit television surveillance of all parking areas
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Parking ratio of 1 space per 25 square meters; Automated Number Plate Recognition entry gates
  • Minimum 10 percent of spaces equipped with electric vehicle charging points
  • Covered bicycle storage with adjacent shower and changing facilities
⚡
ელ. ენერგია და სარეზერვო სისტემა
● Mandatory
  • 100 percent backup power capacity via on-site diesel generator sized for the entire building load
  • Uninterruptible Power Supply with minimum 15-minute autonomy for all critical systems
  • Generator activation and full load assumption within 15 seconds of mains failure
  • Electrical distribution capacity of 100 to 150 volt-amperes per square meter
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Dual utility feed from two independent grid supply points, eliminating single points of failure
  • Uninterruptible Power Supply autonomy of 30 minutes or more; total generator capacity of 2 megavolt-amperes or greater
  • Online energy monitoring via Building Management System or SCADA
🔒
უსაფრთხოება და წვდომის კონტროლი
● Mandatory
  • 24-hour, seven-days-a-week on-site security guard with full closed-circuit television coverage of all common areas, corridors, and parking
  • Magnetic card or Radio-Frequency Identification access control at all building entry points and floor-level doors
  • Fully operational automatic fire sprinkler system throughout all occupied and common areas
  • Minimum video footage retention period of 30 days
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Biometric access control (facial recognition or fingerprint) at primary lobby entry points
  • Integrated security management platform with centralized remote monitoring and incident reporting
  • Video footage retention of 60 days or more
🌐
ტელეკომუნიკაცია და IT ინფრასტრუქტურა
● Mandatory
  • Fiber optic internet connectivity from a minimum of two independent telecommunications operators, entering via separate routes
  • Category 6A structured cabling pre-installed throughout all office floors as part of the base building specification
  • Complete in-building mobile coverage for all major network operators
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Fifth-generation (5G) mobile network repeater infrastructure
  • Dedicated Main Distribution Frame room at ground level and Intermediate Distribution Frame rooms on each floor
  • Online Building Management System and Energy Management System dashboard accessible by tenants
🌿
ენერგოეფექტიანობა და მწვანე სერტიფიკაცია
● Mandatory
  • Building energy performance at or above European Energy Performance Certificate Class B standard
  • Minimum 95 percent LED lighting in all common areas and base-build installations
  • Annual independent energy audit with accuracy within ±5 kilowatt-hours per square meter per year
◎ Desirable — A+
  • LEED Silver certification or higher, or BREEAM "Very Good" rating or higher
  • Low-emissivity high-performance double or triple glazed curtain wall system
  • DALI-2 digital addressable lighting control system with daylight harvesting and occupancy sensing
  • Rooftop solar photovoltaic installation
🏛️
ლობი, საერთო სივრცეები და ტენანტების სერვისები
● Mandatory
  • Representative ground-floor reception lobby of at least 100 square meters with staffed reception desk and concierge service
  • On-site or adjacent café or restaurant facility
  • Well-maintained restroom facilities on every occupied floor
  • Barrier-free accessible entrances and elevator access for persons with reduced mobility
◎ Desirable — A+
  • On-site fitness center available to building tenants
  • Full compliance with Americans with Disabilities Act guidelines or equivalent international standard
  • Rooftop terrace or landscaped outdoor amenity space
  • Shared conference and meeting room suite available on a booking basis
🏢
მართვა და საოპერაციო სტანდარტები
● Mandatory
  • Dedicated full-time professional property management company; building must not be self-managed
  • Service Level Agreement guaranteeing response to emergency calls within 4 hours and routine requests within 48 hours
  • Permanent on-site management offices; annual independent building and systems inspection
◎ Desirable — A+
  • Property management company holding ISO 41001 Facility Management certification
  • Management by a globally recognized operator: CBRE, Cushman & Wakefield, JLL, or Savills
  • Tenant digital self-service portal for requests, billing, and building information
  • Integrated fire safety, emergency, and security systems with 24-hour centralized monitoring
📊
A კლასის დონეების შედარება
  • A+: All mandatory requirements, plus LEED Silver or BREEAM "Very Good" certification, floor plate ≥ 1,500 m², dual utility feed, Destination Dispatch elevator system, and ≥ 10% electric vehicle-ready parking
  • A: All mandatory requirements: Variable Refrigerant Flow / Variable Refrigerant Volume heating, ventilation, and air conditioning with Building Management System, floor plate ≥ 1,000 m², parking ratio 1:30–40
  • A−: Meets minimum Class A technical specification with documented limitations in location, building age, or floor plate size
  • B/A: Meets Class A specification in some areas but classified as Class B due to location, age, or overall specification constraints
Class B Office Building — Classification Standard
Class B encompasses functional, well-maintained buildings that fall short of Class A thresholds but are clearly distinguished from speculative or unrefurbished stock. Often a former Class A building that has aged in place, or a new building with a secondary location. Mandatory — the minimum requirement for Class B classification; Desirable — Class B+ standard or best practice.

Source: CBRE Georgia Office Market Report 2024 · Cushman & Wakefield Global Office Classification Standards · BOMA International

ადგილმდებარეობა და ხელმისაწვდომობა
● Mandatory
  • Located in a recognized business zone or primary commercial corridor
  • Public transportation accessible within a 15-minute walk
  • Adequate on-site or immediately adjacent parking available to tenants
◎ Desirable — B+
  • Walking distance to the nearest metro station of 10 minutes or less
  • Prominent frontage on or direct visibility from a main commercial street
  • Proximity to retail, banking, and food service amenities
📐
სართულის ფართობი და ჭერის სიმაღლე
● Mandatory
  • Minimum net rentable floor area of 400 square meters
  • Clear ceiling height of at least 2.5 meters
  • Layout compatible with standard office fit-out
◎ Desirable — B+
  • Floor plate of 600 square meters or more
  • Clear ceiling height of 2.7 meters or greater
  • Open-plan layout with minimal structural columns
❄️
გათბობა, ვენტილაცია და კონდიციონირება
● Mandatory
  • Split-unit, cassette-type, or Variable Refrigerant Flow air conditioning covering all rentable areas
  • Mechanical fresh air supply on each occupied floor
  • Temperature range maintained between 20 and 24 degrees Celsius
◎ Desirable — B+
  • Centralized or full Variable Refrigerant Flow / Variable Refrigerant Volume system with individual zone control
  • Building Management System with 24-hour, seven-days-a-week automated monitoring
  • Fresh air supply rate of not less than 8 liters per second per person
🛗
ლიფტები და ვერტიკალური ტრანსპორტი
● Mandatory
  • At least one fully operational elevator required in any building of four or more stories
  • Elevator maintained under an active service contract; average waiting time not exceeding 60 seconds
◎ Desirable — B+
  • Equipment from a recognized European manufacturer: KONE, Otis, or Schindler
  • Travel speed of at least 1.0 meter per second
  • Dedicated service or freight elevator separate from passenger lifts
🅿️
პარკინგი
● Mandatory
  • On-site surface-level or underground parking available to tenants
  • Minimum parking ratio of 1 space per 100 square meters of net rentable area
◎ Desirable — B+
  • Underground or covered parking; ratio of 1 space per 50 square meters or better
  • Closed-circuit television surveillance of all parking areas
  • At least one electric vehicle charging point
⚡
ელ. ენერგია და სარეზერვო სისტემა
● Mandatory
  • Standby diesel generator providing power to all common areas, shared facilities, and information technology infrastructure
  • Uninterruptible Power Supply system protecting the server room and critical tenant equipment
◎ Desirable — B+
  • 100 percent floor-level backup power coverage; dual utility feed from two independent supply points
  • Uninterruptible Power Supply with minimum 10-minute autonomy for all floors
  • Remote online power consumption monitoring and sub-metering system
🔒
უსაფრთხოება და წვდომის კონტროლი
● Mandatory
  • Electronic access control at all building entry points, operated by card, PIN code, or mobile application
  • Closed-circuit television surveillance in all common areas and building entrances
  • Fully operational fire detection, alarm, and emergency notification system
◎ Desirable — B+
  • 24-hour, seven-days-a-week on-site security guard or staffed reception presence
  • Radio-Frequency Identification access control system
  • Minimum video footage retention period of 14 days
🌐
ტელეკომუნიკაცია და IT ინფრასტრუქტურა
● Mandatory
  • Fiber optic internet connectivity from a minimum of two independent telecommunications operators
  • Minimum Category 5e structured cabling pre-installed throughout all office floors
  • Adequate in-building coverage for major mobile telephone network operators
◎ Desirable — B+
  • Fiber optic distribution point on each individual office floor
  • Category 6 or higher structured cabling as the floor base build standard
  • Dedicated, lockable telecommunications equipment room on each floor
🌿
ენერგოეფექტიანობა
● Mandatory
  • Adequate thermal insulation of the building envelope meeting applicable construction energy codes
  • Double-glazed windows throughout; energy-efficient lighting in common areas compliant with European Union energy standards
◎ Desirable — B+
  • Minimum 70 percent LED lighting in all common areas; building energy performance at European Energy Performance Certificate Class C or better
  • Building Management System or electrical sub-metering for continuous energy monitoring
🏛️
ლობი, საერთო სივრცეები და ტენანტების სერვისები
● Mandatory
  • Functional entrance lobby with staffed or monitored reception or security desk
  • Clean, well-maintained restroom facilities on each occupied floor
  • Adequate artificial lighting in all common areas, staircases, and corridors
◎ Desirable — B+
  • On-site café, shared kitchenette, or adjacent food service facility
  • Dedicated on-site building management or service office accessible to tenants
  • Weather-protected main building entrance (canopy, porch, or covered drop-off zone)
🏢
მართვა და საოპერაციო სტანდარტები
● Mandatory
  • Professional property management with a clearly identified point of contact and responsive maintenance team
  • Regular upkeep of all common areas, shared systems, and building exterior envelope
  • Routine maintenance requests addressed within a documented timeframe
◎ Desirable — B+
  • Management by a certified property management company with a formal Service Level Agreement guaranteeing routine response within 72 hours
  • Annual building inspection and systems audit; documented preventive maintenance schedule
📊
B კლასის დონეების შედარება
  • B+: All mandatory requirements, plus centralized or full Variable Refrigerant Flow heating, ventilation, and air conditioning with Building Management System, floor plate ≥ 600 m², parking ratio 1:50, Radio-Frequency Identification access control, and 100% backup power coverage
  • B: All mandatory requirements: floor plate ≥ 400 m², at least one elevator (4+ stories), basic air conditioning, electronic access control, closed-circuit television in common areas
  • B/A: Near Class A technical specification; classified as Class B due to location or age constraints — typically a former Class A building
